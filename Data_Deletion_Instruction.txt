At Kip Shopping, we respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring the protection of your personal data. If you wish to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion
To request the deletion of your data from our systems, follow these steps:

Log in to Your Account:

Visit our website or open the app and log in to your account using your credentials.
Navigate to Account Settings:

After logging in, go to the "Account Settings" or "Profile Settings" section.
Request Data Deletion:

In the "Account Settings" section, you will find an option labeled "Delete My Data" or "Request Data Deletion."
Click on this option and follow the prompts to submit your data deletion request.
Submit a Manual Request (If No Account or Issues Logging In):

If you do not have access to your account or are facing issues, you can submit a manual request by emailing us at mauryasuraja2504@gmail.com.
In your email, please provide:
Your full name
The email address associated with your account
The reason for your data deletion request
Confirmation:

Once we receive your request, our team will process it and delete your personal data within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
Important Information
Deleting your data means you will lose access to your account and all related services.
Any ongoing subscriptions or services associated with your account will be terminated once your data is deleted.
If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team at mauryasuraja2504@gmail.com.
